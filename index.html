About

Description

The goal of the game is to survive in the streets of New York, collect emerging items, kill the boss and as many zombies as possible

Technologies

This game uses: JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, Web Audio API

Difficulty

You can choose the difficulty suitable for your level of play: Baby Mode, Normal Mode or Hell Mode

Controls

Movement hero can by controlled using the buttons W A D or with the Arrows Up Left and Right. Buttons SPACE attack with a sword and E throw kunai, allow you to kill zombies. By pressing the S button or the Down Arrow, the hero makes a sliding tackle, after which the zombies fall and after some time they rise.
